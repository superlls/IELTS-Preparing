# Section 4 Review: Food Marketing Trends

## 这些词在原文中的出现位置

### is obsessed with
> "The food industry in the UK, in particular, **is obsessed with** finding and exploiting the next big food trend."
- 出现在开场，说食品行业对新趋势的态度

### previously
> "Marketeers aim to create a huge demand for a food item, which was **previously** unknown, or not needed."
- 形容该食品在过去的状态（之前不为人知）

### vegan
> "Companies which sell **vegan** produce were pioneers in being able to increase sales really quickly in this way."
- 指素食产品

### pioneers
> "Companies which sell vegan produce were **pioneers** in being able to increase sales really quickly in this way."
- 指"先驱者"，是这种营销模式的最早采用者

### dedicated
> "They have **dedicated** teams closely following which new products or ingredients are trending on social media..."
- 形容有专门的团队负责这项工作

### profile
> "A British PR company was hired to raise its **profile** and stimulate demand."
- **关键短语**：raise one's profile = 提高知名度/形象

### staple
> "Articles written following this visit helped to educate the British public about the avocado, which at this time was certainly not the daily **staple** it's since become."
- 指"日常必需品"，牛油果后来成为了日常食品

### therefore
> "Advertisements were designed to promote the avocado as a 'superfood': rich in nutrients and **therefore** beneficial for health."
- **关键词！！！** 答案词通常跟在 therefore 之后，或者 therefore 标记了逻辑关联点
- **用户特别标注**：放到核心方法笔记里！

### campaigns
> "Avocados became hugely fashionable and within a few years UK avocado sales had grown from 13 million pounds annually to around 150 million, making it one of the most successful fresh produce **campaigns** in UK history."
- 指营销"活动"

### provocation
> "They attracted a lot of attention with a media campaign which used **provocation** as a way of getting their message across effectively."
- 指"挑衅"作为传播手段

### aggravated
> "The fact that this campaign **aggravated** competitors producing milk from dairy cows was seen as a plus..."
- 激怒了竞争者

### plus
> "The fact that this campaign aggravated competitors producing milk from dairy cows was seen as a **plus**, as it helped to make oat milk seem cool."
- 这里 plus 作名词，表示"优势"或"好处"

### decided against...in favour of
> "In the USA, the brand **decided against** a big retail launch **in favour of** getting the product into coffee chains..."
- **重点搭配**：对比结构，决定不采取某个方案而选择另一个

### retail
> "In the USA, the brand decided against a big **retail** launch in favour of getting the product into coffee chains..."
- retail = 零售；retail launch = 零售产品上市

### menus
> "Norwegian skrei, a rarely available seasonal fish delicacy, otherwise known as Arctic cod, is now found on the **menus** of Michelin starred restaurants throughout Europe."
- 复数形式，菜单

### is native to
> "This plant **is native to** Peru, and when demand peaked some years ago, the price soared..."
- **短语搭配**：is native to = 原产于

### soared
> "This plant is native to Peru, and when demand peaked some years ago, the price **soared**, making it unaffordable for local people."
- price soared = 价格飞涨/暴增

### fertility
> "One issue has been that the **fertility** of the soil decreased dramatically..."
- soil fertility = 土壤肥力

### desertification
> "One issue has been that the fertility of the soil decreased dramatically, which could potentially lead to **desertification** in some areas."
- **最难词**：沙漠化（环境术语）

### encouraged
> "The public can also be **encouraged** to buy things which are more sustainably produced."
- be encouraged to do = 被鼓励去做...

## 改进建议

### 📚 发音激活
- **is obsessed with** - obsessed 的 -ed 要清晰，与 "obsess" 的动词形式区分
- **previously** - 这是副词，-ly 结尾，容易在快速语流中漏掉整个词，要特别激活
- **vegan** - VEE-gan，不是 vay-gan，这个发音容易被说错
- **pioneers** - 重音在第二音节 pio-NEER-s，不要听成 IONE-eers
- **dedicated** - DED-i-kay-ted，末尾 -ed 必须听清，不能吞掉
- **profile** - PRO-file，这个词在商务英语中很常见
- **staple** - STA-ple，与另一含义"订书钉"的发音相同，但上下文辨义
- **therefore** - THERE-fore，这个词虽然常见但在快速语流中容易漏掉，**务必激活**
- **campaigns** - cam-PAYN-z，复数末尾 -s 音很关键
- **provocation** - pro-vo-KAY-shun，5音节长词，中间 -vo- 容易吞掉
- **aggravated** - AG-gra-vay-ted，四音节词，-ed 末尾容易被漏掉
- **soared** - SORD，虽然短，但 /ɔ/ 这个元音不常见，容易听错

### 🎯 重点搭配和同义替换
- **raise one's profile** = 提高知名度
- **is obsessed with** = 沉迷于/痴迷于
- **is native to** = 原产于
- **decided against A in favour of B** = 拒绝A而选择B（对比结构，两部分都要听清）
- **price soared** = 价格飞涨
- **soil fertility** = 土壤肥力
- **be encouraged to** = 被鼓励去做...
- **daily staple** = 日常必需品

### ⚡ 下次精听重点
1. **therefore 是关键词** - 这个词标记了逻辑关联，常常意味着答案词或重要信息即将出现。用户特别标注了这个词，说明在这个section中，whenever you hear "therefore"，要特别注意后面的内容
2. **长词处理** - desertification, provocation, aggravated, previously, dedicated, encouraged 都是3-5音节的长词。关键是不要试图听成一个整体，而是分音节听：des-ert-i-fi-CA-tion
3. **短语搭配优先于单词** - raise profile, is native to, decided against...in favour of, price soared 这些都是搭配，要把短语作为一个整体来识别，而不是逐词听
4. **复数形式注意** - pioneers, campaigns, menus 的末尾 -s/-z/-z 要听清，不要漏掉复数信息
5. **对比结构** - "decided against A in favour of B" 这种对比结构中，两边都很重要，第一次听不清可能会miss掉核心信息
